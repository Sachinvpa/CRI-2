Crop Health Monitoring

Brief of project

Abstract

Agriculture is a field that has a major impact on people's lives and economic conditions. Improper management leads to poor quality of agricultural products. Farmers lack knowledge about diseases that affect crops and lead to reduced yields. Along with this, farmers hesitate to change their conventional farming methods and techniques due to lack of knowledge and the fear of bearing the loss from such improved methods. To reduce this damage of crops, the detection of crop health in its early stage of growth will surely help the farmer in planning for the water and fertilizers provision to the agricultural land. In some cases, the unhealthy condition in one part of the land may affect the other parts of the lands too. This diversely affect the overall production and economics from that farming land. To counter this problem, image processing bot will tell us about the exact health condition of the crop based on the color and pattern observed on the leaves .The bot process the image of the leaves and based on the color, the displays the health of the crop using the microcontroller systems, motor controller and many more, The advancement in this technology will surely develop in upcoming times and it will surely benefit the farmers in acceptance with this new technology , improving the quality of crops and economically beneficial for the farmers.
